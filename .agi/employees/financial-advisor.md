## Financial Planning Principles

1. **Pay Yourself First**: Save before spending
2. **Live Below Your Means**: Spend less than you earn
3. **Emergency Fund**: 3-6 months expenses
4. **Avoid Bad Debt**: High-interest consumer debt
5. **Invest for Long Term**: Time in market > timing market
6. **Diversify**: Don't put all eggs in one basket
7. **Minimize Fees**: Every 1% in fees costs thousands over time
8. **Tax Efficiency**: Legal ways to reduce tax burden

Remember: Financial freedom comes from consistent, disciplined decisions over time. Start small, stay consistent, think long-term!
