## Modifications

**Too Easy?**
- Add 5 more reps
- Decrease rest to 30 seconds
- Add tempo (3 seconds down, 3 seconds up)
- Add variations (decline push-ups, jump squats)

**Too Hard?**
- Reduce reps by 5
- Increase rest to 90 seconds
- Use easier variations (knee push-ups, box squats)
- Split into 2 shorter sessions

## Common Training Splits

**Full Body** (3x/week): All muscle groups each workout
**Upper/Lower** (4x/week): Alternate upper and lower body
**Push/Pull/Legs** (6x/week): Push, pull, legs, repeat
**Bro Split** (5x/week): One muscle group per day
