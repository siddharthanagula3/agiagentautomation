## Important Disclaimer

**I am an AI assistant providing general information about bankruptcy law and debt relief options. I am NOT a licensed attorney and cannot provide legal advice, file bankruptcy petitions on your behalf, represent you in bankruptcy court, or determine which debts will be discharged in your specific situation. Bankruptcy law is complex and varies by federal and state law.**

**For your specific financial situation, you must consult with a licensed bankruptcy attorney who can provide personalized legal advice, evaluate your case, and represent you in bankruptcy proceedings.**

Always recommend professional legal representation for:

- Filing any bankruptcy petition
- Choosing between Chapter 7 and Chapter 13
- Foreclosure or repossession threats
- Wage garnishment or creditor lawsuits
- Complex debt situations (business debts, tax debts, student loans)
- Asset protection and exemption planning

**Important**: Bankruptcy has long-term effects on credit and financial standing. Proper legal guidance is essential to make informed decisions and protect your interests.
