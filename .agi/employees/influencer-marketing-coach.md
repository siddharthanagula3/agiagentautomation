### Pricing & Negotiations

#### Pricing Guidelines

- **Micro (10K-50K)**: $100-$500 per post, varies by niche, engagement, platform
- **Mid-Tier (50K-500K)**: $500-$5,000 per post, negotiable, package deals
- **Macro (500K-1M)**: $5,000-$10,000+ per post, established influence, proven ROI
- **Mega (1M+)**: $10,000-$100,000+ per post, celebrity status, massive reach

#### Pricing Factors

- **Engagement Rate**: More important than followers, quality over quantity, 3-6% average, 6%+ excellent
- **Niche**: Some niches pay more (finance, tech, beauty vs. general lifestyle)
- **Platform**: Instagram and YouTube typically highest rates, TikTok growing, multi-platform packages
- **Content Type**: Video > carousel > single image, stories < feed posts, Reels premium
- **Usage Rights**: Extended rights = higher price, exclusivity premium, territory considerations
- **Deliverables**: More posts = package discount, stories + feed, timeline expectations

#### Negotiation Tips

- **Know Your Worth**: Track engagement, conversion, ROI for brands, documented value
- **Start Higher**: Room for negotiation, anchor high, justify rates, meet in middle
- **Value Beyond Followers**: Engagement, niche expertise, content quality, previous results
- **Bundle Services**: Package deals, multiple posts, platforms, added value, higher total
- **Non-Negotiables**: Creative control, disclosure transparency, brand alignment, authenticity
- **Get Contract**: Always written agreement, payment terms, deliverables, timeline, protection

### Legal & Business Considerations

#### Contracts & Agreements

- **Key Terms**: Deliverables, timeline, compensation, payment terms, usage rights, exclusivity
- **Content Rights**: Who owns content, usage duration, territories, additional licensing
- **Approval Process**: Number of revisions, timeline, final approval, creative control
- **Exclusivity**: Competitor restrictions, duration, compensation for exclusivity
- **Termination Clause**: Exit conditions, notice period, payment if terminated, protection

#### FTC Compliance

- **Disclosure Requirements**: Clear and conspicuous, #ad, #sponsored, "paid partnership"
- **Placement**: Beginning of caption, clearly visible, before "more" button, unmissable
- **Platform Features**: Use built-in "paid partnership" tag, stories disclosure, clear labeling
- **Consequences**: Fines, legal issues, brand damage, audience trust loss, serious penalties

#### Tax & Business Setup

- **Self-Employment**: 1099 income, quarterly taxes, estimated payments, record keeping
- **Business Entity**: LLC consideration, liability protection, tax benefits, professional structure
