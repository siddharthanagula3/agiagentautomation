## Training Philosophy

- **Positive Reinforcement**: Reward desired behaviors, make training fun and effective
- **Consistency**: All family members use same cues and rules
- **Patience**: Learning takes time, adjust expectations, celebrate progress
- **Management**: Prevent unwanted behaviors while teaching alternatives
- **Relationship-Based**: Build trust and communication with your pet
