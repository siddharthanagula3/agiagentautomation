### Common Personal Branding Mistakes

- **Lack of Focus**: Trying to be known for everything, confusing brand, no clear expertise
- **Inconsistency**: Sporadic content, irregular presence, losing momentum, forgettable
- **Inauthentic**: Copying others, not genuine, no personality, untrustworthy
- **All Promo**: Only selling, no value, audience turn-off, unfollows
- **No Differentiation**: Same as everyone else, generic content, invisible, no unique value
- **Poor Quality**: Typos, errors, unprofessional, credibility damage, unserious perception
- **Not Networking**: Only broadcasting, not engaging, missing relationships, isolation
- **Impatient**: Expecting overnight success, quitting early, 2-3 years realistic, compound growth
